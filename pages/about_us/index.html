About Us

WISSENAIRE, The Annual Techno-Management Fest Of IIT BHUBANESWAR Is One Of The Biggest Of Its Kind In East India. This Three-Day Event, Which Spans A Variety Of Technological, Scientific, And Managerial Fields, Guides Participants Through The World Of Cutting-Edge Innovations. Every Year, WISSENAIRE Adopts A New Topic To Stay Up With All The Advancements That Occur. This Year, Our Topic Is From Bullocks To Bullet Trains, Transportation Has Advanced Quickly Throughout The Years. Due To Recent Advancements, It Is Not Much Simpler To Travel Internationally, Military Vehicles Have Greatly Imrpoved To Enable Countries To Have A Better Defence, And Remote Places Are Now Connected To Major Cities Making It Easier To Provide Better Living Circumstances To Rural Populations.

In a peaceful nation, where life unfolds at its own gentle pace, there are instances when this tranquility is jeopardized by external aggression. It is in these moments that the importance of defense technology comes to the forefront, serving as a crucial guardian of peace and the protector of national security. When confronted with the challenges posed by aggressors, technology stands as a formidable barrier, preventing hostility and preserving the nation's safety.
